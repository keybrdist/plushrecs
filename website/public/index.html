Est. 2009

Founded in 2009, Plush Recordings is a U.S. based record label featuring a worlwide roster of talent including works by artists who push the boundaries of modern break-beat oriented music from atmospheric and ambient jungle, to retro liquid funk. Some of our top talent includes 360 joYnt, Earl Grey, Utah Jazz, martianMan, Samurai Breaks, robbyt, Doctor Jeep, Balistik, Stunna, B Fuse, No Rules, Quentin Hiatus, and a slew of amazing artists who dedicate their life to music.

Our Roster

Plush Recordings is home to a diverse collective of innovative electronic music producers. Our roster represents both established veterans and emerging talents who are pushing the boundaries of drum & bass, jungle, and atmospheric electronic music.

Interactive Art & Simulations

Hello everyone. This is a message from Mike Shoebox. I am working on a series of new pixel-art animations and interactive designs (through-code), to sync to all the amazing music we are releasing on PLUSH. These interactive demos will be available strictly on this website, because this is the only way we can host them publicly in the way they were meant to be experienced. If you notice any videos on social media of me showing off these demos, make sure you click through to the website to experience them in their full glory.

My only goal with these demos is to create something cool and unique to go along with these sounds we all love so much. I hope you like them!
