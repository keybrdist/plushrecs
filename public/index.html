About Us

Founded in 2009, Plush Recordings is a U.S. based record label featuring a worlwide roster of talent including works by artist who push the boundaries of modern break-beat oriented music from atmospheric and ambient jungle, to retro liquid funk. Some of our top talent includes 360 joYnt, Earl Grey, Utah Jazz, martianMan, Samurai Breaks, robbyt, Doctor Jeep, Balistik, Stunna, B Fuse, No Rules, Quentin Hiatus, and a slew of amazing artists who dedicate their life to music.

Our mission is to provide a platform for emerging and established artists to showcase their unique styles, contributing to the evolution of the Jungle and Drum & Bass genres. Plush Recordings is home to a diverse catalog of releases that capture the essence of deep, immersive, and high-quality electronic music.

Interactive Art

Hello everyone. This is a message from Mike Shoebox. I am working on a series of new pixel-art animations and interactive designs (through-code), to sync to all the amazing music we are releasing on PLUSH. These interactive demos will be available strictly on this website, because this is the only way we can host them publicly in the way they were meant to be experienced. If you notice any videos on social media of me showing off these demos, make sure you click through to the website to experience them in their full glory.

My only goal with these demos is to create something cool and unique to go along with these sounds we all love so much. I hope you like them!
