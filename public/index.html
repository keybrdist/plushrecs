Plush Recordings

About Us

Founded in 2009, **Plush Recordings** is a Denver-based record label dedicated to delivering soulful and atmospheric Jungle and Drum & Bass music. With a focus on fresh sounds and innovative production, Plush Recordings has established itself as a key player in the global electronic music scene.

Our mission is to provide a platform for emerging and established artists to showcase their unique styles, contributing to the evolution of the Jungle and Drum & Bass genres. Plush Recordings is home to a diverse catalog of releases that capture the essence of deep, immersive, and high-quality electronic music.

Our Music

Plush Recordings has built a rich discography over the years, including standout releases such as:

  • Vybryda - Echoes EP
  • Altered Perception - Void EP
  • Shivaxi - Sun Daze / Ambient Sphere
  • Clima - Proper Thing / Only For Deep Souls
